# Finding the Perfect Contractor Match

Selecting the right contractor is crucial for project success. Here are key factors to consider:

## 1. Verify Credentials
- Check licenses and insurance
- Review certifications
- Verify bonding status

## 2. Evaluate Experience
- Years in business
- Similar project portfolio
- Specializations

## 3. Check References
- Recent client feedback
- Project completion history
- Communication style

## Red Flags to Watch For
- Pressure to decide quickly
- No written contracts
- Unusually low bids
- Requests for full upfront payment
